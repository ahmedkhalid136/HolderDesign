Welcome To Holder Enterprises

Holder Enterprises, Inc. is a DC based, minority owned, certified small disadvantaged established in 2004. Our primary purpose is food service management, training solutions and temporary support services. Over the years, our company has grown into one of the most respected food services providers due to our strict adherence to quality, nutrition, sanitation, and training to prepare and supply high quality food as per the requirements of our clients. Our Temporary support services allow agencies immediate access to pre-screened and qualified support staffing when required. Holder Enterprises, Inc. helps our clients with having the result of a professionally run organization, operating within its financial blueprint, that allows its employees to focus on core functions that deliver genuine value.

The Holder Advantage uniquely combines technology, quality assurance, reliability, USDA/FDA certified high quality food supply chain management, superior personnel, temporary support staffing and continuous training to ensure our clients receive superior services.
